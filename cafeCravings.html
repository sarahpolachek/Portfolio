Project Overview

Overall Goal

The development of a menu brochure that will provide customers with a valuable and easy-to-read layout as well as a size designed for use on-the-go.

Key Features

  • Easy to take on the go.
  • An appropriate brochure size, about the size of printer paper 8.5"x11" folded in thirds.
  • Menu items organized in appropriate sections with proper item names and prices.
  • Easy to read and follow along with menu items.

Target Audience

  • Customers at the café:
  • Regulars who want to take the menu on the go.
  • New customers who want to see what the café has to offer, especially if they want to order over the phone.
