Project Overview


Overall Goal


Reach the attention of an audience interested in martial arts, self-defense, and exercise.

Provide value in the visual they see that will increase their desire to learn Jiu Jitsu through the storyline of video clips as well as title text throughout the video that will provide information on the benefits of Jiu Jitsu.

Understanding the Audience


  • In order to improve the recording experience and moments captured by understanding what will appeal to audiences
  • Utilize feedback from potential students as well as parents on what they're looking for in a dojo and what they'd like to see training at Indio.

Visualizations


  • Tell the story through video and text of a day at Indio Dojo for Jiu Jitsu.

Question


Men, women, and children alike feel they would be more confident if they knew a form of self defense and had more confidence. However, they are worried about being intimidated by a martial arts gym where injuries can sometimes be common.

Many individuals are also consistently looking for a way to get in shape, yet loose motivation to go to the gym; where they aren't sure what types of exercises will benefit them.

Jiu Jitsu offers individuals the opportunity to get in shape while learning a self defense.

Can Jiu Jitsu provide a safe, family-friendly space to train?

Finding Data


Though there are many articles written on self defense and the benefits, it can be an intimidating class to take or sport to start.

After interviewing individuals interested in self defense and Jiu Jitsu, as well as parents and their children; a prominent concern was the friendless of a dojo and the safety of the individual while training there.

Summarizing the Story


Target User


Individuals looking to get in shape and learn Jiu Jitsu. Parents looking to enroll their child to build confidence.

Provides user's with a walk through of what to expect when taking a class at Indio Dojo.
