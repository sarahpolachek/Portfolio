About

Throughout my life, I have always jumped at any chance to be creative. I loved creating greeting cards and filling up sketch pads. When it came time to think about furthering my education and what I may enjoy doing, it seemed like a no brainer to do something in design. During this time, I found that I enjoy making websites and I questioned how I could combine creativity with web design.


UX was a new topic to me, when I read about it halfway through my bachelors. It was something that piqued my interest. I had received my Associates in Psychology and thought that the field of UX would allow me to utilize my interest in people with my love for design. After graduating, I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; so that I could better my knowledge in design and speak to developers more fluently.


Through this journey, I have found myself encompassed in people, businesses, and communities, and the heart that beats within each one. I've grown into a person who loves web design and has a growth mindset, constantly eager to learn new things. Visually, I love the layout, the organization, and the production of how a website will look and function. Technically, I want to dive into the how and why behind a users actions and how to best develop the website or application for those actions. Taking a visual approach through design and content creation is essential to place emphasis on how users will engage with your product.


In my free time, I enjoy photography as well as video creation. This gives me the opportunity to piece together a story and figure out the best way to present it so that it expresses a question, a feeling, or a call to action.
