Sherpa 6

My work at Sherpa 6 as an Associate UX/UI Engineer is protected and covered by a Secret Security Clearance. Currently, I am unable to speak upon or display any of my project work. However, I will do my best to provide a brief of the processes I have used.

I help envision the user experience and work to bring an experience to life. By approaching problems with a curious mind, I apply user-focused design solutions to develop functional and effective designs.

The project I am currently working on began with user research and defining user needs. During this process, my team was able to pull together focus group, where we have been able to discuss user and system needs as well as use cases with veterans who have worked in the field before. Using the knowledge gathered within focus group meetings, I work to build out wireframes that display the needs of the user and define the requirements of the system.

I then present the designs to the software team and we discuss the feasibility of the designs and other viable paths for the interface. Once I've reviewed with the software team, I make any necessary changes and then I then decide if a further focus group meeting is needed. This process is repeated until designs are approved and initially submitted for development.

After wireframes are sent to the software team, I then build out the high-fidelity prototypes that will give the developers exact measurements and definitive flows. I then begin the process again for the next sprint.

Timer Activity Challenge

The concept of this app was to create a quick timer that users could use to time their workouts.

Users have the ability to choose a workout, choose an amount of time for working out, view how much time has passed, and how many calories were burned.

Intranet - Microsoft Sharepoint

This project cannot be shared publicly, however, I helped build Victorian Finance's internal employee website.

This project was based in Microsoft Sharepoint and was handed off to the company with skeleton wireframes. My role in this project consisted of helping fill in and build out each webpage using wireframes and css customization.

The goal was to create a similar look and feel across the site by using branded colors and fonts and making sure the placement of content and elements reflected similarly across each page.
